The GREATEST Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

GURANTEED the BEST Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of grape jelly

Directions

  1. On one slice of bread, spread the peanut butter evenly over the bread
  2. On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly evenly over the bread
  3. Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in
  4. Enjoy!
