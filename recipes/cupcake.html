Nothing Special Cupcake

A cupcake that is Nothing Special...

Ingredients

  • Car or legs (preferably a car)
  • Grocery store or supermarket near you
  • Money $$$
  • The Cupcakes

Directions

  1. Go to the store using your car or your legs
  2. Grab the cupcakes from the bakery or from the shelves if you don't have one
  3. Purchase the cupcakes with your money
  4. Enjoy the cupcakes while walking or in your car!
